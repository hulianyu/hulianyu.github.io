About me

I received my Ph.D. degree in Software Engineering from Dalian University of Technology, China, in June 2025, supervised by Prof. Zengyou He. Previously, I obtained my M.Sc. degree in Computer Science from Ningbo University, China, in 2019.
My research interests include Machine Learning, Data Mining, and Causal Inference.
