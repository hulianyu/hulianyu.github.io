About me

I received the M.Sc. degree in computer science from Ningbo University, China, in 2019.
I am currently working toward a Ph.D. degree (expected to graduate in June 2025) under the supervision of Prof. Zengyou He at Dalian University of Technology.
My research interests primarily focus on machine learning and data mining.
